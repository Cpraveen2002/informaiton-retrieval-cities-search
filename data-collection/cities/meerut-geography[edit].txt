meerut
Geography[edit]
geography[edit]

for description of the geography of the district, see meerut district § geography.
meerut is the largest city in ncr after delhi also known as sports city of india. meerut lies between the plains of the ganges and those of the yamuna. in area meerut district covers 2,522 km (974 sq mi), which is larger than delhi (delhi covers an area of 1,484 km [573 sq mi]). however, meerut's population is three times less than that of delhi (current population of meerut is 3,443,689).
