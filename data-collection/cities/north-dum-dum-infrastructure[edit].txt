north-dum-dum
Infrastructure[edit]
infrastructure[edit]
as per the district census handbook 2011, north dumdum municipal city covered an area of 26.45 km (10.21 sq mi). amongst the civic amenities it had both open and closed drains. amongst the educational facilities it had 56 primary schools and 18 secondary schools. amongst the social, recreational and cultural facilities it had 1 auditorium/ community hall and 3 public libraries. it had 9 bank branches.
