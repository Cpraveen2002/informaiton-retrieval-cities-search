media and communications[edit]
state-owned all india radio has a local station and has fm channel 102.4 vividh bharati in rajkot which transmits various programs of mass interest. rajkot also  has private local news media  and digital website. private fm stations like radio mirchi 98.3 fm, red fm 93.5, big 92.7 fm and 94.3 my fm also serve the people of rajkot with excellent entertainment and events.
