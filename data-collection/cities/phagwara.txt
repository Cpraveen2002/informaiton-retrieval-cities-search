phagwara is a city and municipal corporation in kapurthala district in punjab, india. it lies on national highway 44 and located 40 kilometres from kapurthala, the district headquarter, 124 kilometres (77 mi) away from chandigarh, 20 kilometres (12 mi) away from jalandhar and 361 kilometres (224 mi) from new delhi. the city is famous for sugar, glucose, starch, fine fabric textiles, and auto parts manufacturing for engines in punjab. this place is also known for the shri hanuman garhi temple, which has statues of the hindu god, lord hanuman.
