Demographics[edit]
demographics[edit]
as of 2011 india census, faridkot had a population of 618,008 which constitute about 2% of the total population of punjab. males constitute 327,121 of the population and females 290,887.the population density in this district is 424 persons per km. faridkot has an average literacy rate of 70.6%: male literacy is 75.9%, and female literacy is 64.8%.
