buxar is a nagar parishad city in the state of bihar, india bordering uttar pradesh. it is the headquarters of the eponymous buxar district, as well as the headquarters of the community development block of buxar, which also contains the census town of sarimpur along with 132 rural villages.
in modern times, the historic battle of chausa and battle of buxar were fought in the vicinity. buxar railway station lies on patna–mughalsarai section of howrah–delhi main line. it is approximately 125 km from the state capital of patna. the local language of buxar is bhojpuri.
