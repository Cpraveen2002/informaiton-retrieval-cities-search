cuttack
Etymology[edit]
etymology[edit]
the name cuttack is an anglicised form of kataka (odia: କଟକ) which is derived from sanskrit meaning capital, fort and military establishment or a cantonment. the city was known as bidanasi katak (meaning bidanasi military base) during the days when barabati fort was in existence. bidanasi is now one of the localities of the city.
