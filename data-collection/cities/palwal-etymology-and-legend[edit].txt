etymology and legend[edit]
the origin of palwal is related to the local legend related to the mahabharata, according to which the town is named after an asura called "palwasura" who was slain by balarama, the brother of krishna. to commemorate this event an annual festival called "baldev chhat ka mela" is held here, and a "dauji temple" in honor of balrama is situated in banchari village 25Â km from palwal on grand trunk road.
