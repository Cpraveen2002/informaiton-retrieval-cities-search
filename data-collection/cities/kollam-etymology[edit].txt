etymology[edit]
in 825 ce, the malayalam calendar, or kollavarsham, was created in kollam at meetings held in the city. the present malayalam calendar is said to have begun with the re-founding of the town, which was rebuilt after its destruction by fire. the name kollam is believed to have been derived from the sanskrit word kollam, meaning pepper.
