demographics[edit]
as of 2011 census of india, thanesar had a population of 154,962. males constitute 55% of the population and females 45% (83,655 – 71,307). thanesar has an average literacy rate of 85.73%, higher than the national average of 74.04: male literacy is 89.89%, and female literacy is 80.85%. in thanesar, 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
