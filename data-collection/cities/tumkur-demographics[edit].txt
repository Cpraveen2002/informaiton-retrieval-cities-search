Demographics[edit]
demographics[edit]
as of 2011 india census, tumkur had a population of 305,821. more than 5,00,000 people lived in the corporation limit. at the 2001 census, males constitutes 52% of the population and females 48%. tumkur had an average literacy rate of 75%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy was 79%, and female literacy was 70%.  about 11% of the population was under 6 years of age. the most common language in the city is kannada.
