medinirai medical college and hospital dav institute of engineering and technology established by d.a.v college managing committee. bhishma narain singh law college ganesh lal agrawal college yodh singh namdhari mahila mahavidyalaya janta shivratri college elite public b.ed. college jyoti prakash mahila b.ed. college

m.k. dav public school, chianki (cbse) sacred heart school, chianki (icse) heritage international school (cbse)
