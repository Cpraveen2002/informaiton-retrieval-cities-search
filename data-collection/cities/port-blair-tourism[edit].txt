tourism[edit]
port blair being the capital city of andaman and nicobar islands is the entry point for all tourism related activities. you will first have to arrive to port blair before moving any other islands in andaman. at port blair, the major tourist places to visit is the cellular jail, corbyns cove beach, north bay island, ross island renamed to netaji subhash chandra bose island, chidiatapu, wandoor and other museums that are present within city limits. entry charges are applicable to some of the tourist places.
