bhimavaram is a city and headquarters of west godavari district of the andhra pradesh state of india. it is the administrative headquarters of bhimavaram mandal. as of 2011 census, it is the most populous urban area in the district with a population of 210,000. it is one of the major pilgrimage centers in the state, which is home to somaramam, one of the five great pancharama kshetras.
