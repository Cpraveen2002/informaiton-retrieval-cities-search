origin of name[edit]
the city was named daltonganj during the british raj after irishman colonel edward tuite dalton (1815â€“1880), an anthropologist and the commissioner of chota nagpur in 1861.  the name was changed to medininagar in 2004 by the state government of jharkhand.  the former name is still retained in the name of the city's railway station.  it is administered by the medininagar municipal corporation, which was formed on 30 may 2015.   the city is named after raja medini ray of chero dynasty.
