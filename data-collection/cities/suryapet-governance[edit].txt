suryapet municipality was constituted in 1952. it was upgraded to grade–ii in 1984, grade–i on 7 november 1998. the area of the municipality is spread over an area of 54.00 km (20.85 sq mi) with 48 wards. the present municipal commissioner is v. surender and the chairman is perumalla annapurna.
the city also serves as the headquarters of suryapet mandal in the suryapet revenue division.
