Transportation[edit]
transportation[edit]
rail[edit]
malerkotla is situated on delhi-jakhal-dhuri-ludhiana railway line. the nearest railway junctions are dhuri (18 km [11 mi]) and ludhiana 45 km [28 mi]).
air[edit]
the nearest airports to malerkotla are:
chandigarh international airport, sahibzada ajit singh nagar, mohali (about 120 km (75 mi) away) sri guru ram dass jee international airport, amritsar (about 250 km (160 mi) away) sahnewal airport (iata: luh, icao: vild), also known as ludhiana airport (about 49.8 km (30.9 mi) (66min) away)
