Transport[edit]
transport[edit]
railway stations
there are 3 railway stations in bhilai city, bhilai railway station, bhilai nagar railway station & bhilai power house railway station.
bus
the durg bus stand is a hub where you can find any bus to any place in and around chhattisgarh as well as other parts of the country.
airways
the nearest major airport is swami vivekananda international airport, raipur, which is 50km far away from here.
