bardhaman (/ˈbɔːrdəˌmɑːn/, bengali: [ˈbɔrˌd̪ʱo.man]) is a city and a municipality in the state of west bengal, india. it is the headquarters of purba bardhaman district, having become a district capital during the period of british rule. burdwan, an alternative name for the city, has remained in use since then.
