naihati is a city and a municipality of north 24 parganas district in the indian state of west bengal. it is a part of the area covered by kolkata metropolitan development authority (kmda).
naihati municipality is one of the oldest in the whole country and was established in 1869. it is the birthplace of bankim chandra chattopadhyay, the author of india's national song vande mataram.
