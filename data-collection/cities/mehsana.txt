mehsana (pronunciation (help·info)), also spelled mahesana, is a city and municipality in mehsana district, in the indian state of gujarat. established in 14th century, the city was under gaekwads of baroda state from 18th century to the independence of india in 1947. the municipality was established in 1919–20. the town has population of about 185,000. dairy, oil and natural gas are major industries while there are several small and medium enterprises in the city.
