Demography[edit]
demography[edit]
as of 2012 india census, kirari suleman nagar has a population of 154633 (2001). males constitute 55% of the population and females 45%. kirari suleman nagar has an average literacy rate of 49%, less than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 51%, and female literacy is 38%. in kirari suleman nagar, 20% of the population is under 6 years of age. this is a census town in north west district in the indian state of delhi. kirari suleman nagar approved residency by aam aadmi party in 2017.
