Administration[edit]
administration[edit]
municipality office of hosur
hosur was constituted as a selection grade town panchayat in 1962. it was upgraded to second grade municipality in the year 1992. in 1998, it was upgraded to selection grade municipality vide g.o.(maws) no.85 dated 22 may 1998. in 2011, vide go. no. 127 dated 8 september 2011 town panchayat of mathigiri and village panchayats of zuzuvadi, mookandapalli, avalapalli and chennathur were included in hosur municipality. on 13 february 2019, hosur was upgraded as the 13th corporation city of tamil nadu comprising the adjoining areas.
