the 2011 census, recorded a population of 166,537. the total population constituted 82,743 males and 83,794 females. these numbers produced a gender ratio of 1013 females per 1000 males, which was higher than the national average of 940 per 1000. in the same year, 18,406 children were between 0 and 6 years of age. in this group, 9,355 were boys and 9,051 were girls, giving a gender ratio of 968 per 1000. the average literacy rate was 68.38 percent with 101,292 literate people. this number was significantly lower than the national average of 73 percent.
the urban agglomeration had a population of 184,771, of which 92,006 were male and 92,765 were female, giving a gender ratio of 1008 females per 1000 males. children aged 0 to 6 years numbered 20,517. there were 112,151 literate people giving an average literacy rate of 68.28 percent.
telugu is an official language of the city. kannada and urdu are also widely spoken.
