education[edit]
the primary and secondary school education is imparted by the government, aided and private schools, under the school education department of the state. railway high school and sjp govt school are government schools, and kc. narayana, rotary, and st. peters, are private schools. the medium of instruction followed by different schools are english and telugu. for guntakal updates you can visit managuntakal.in
