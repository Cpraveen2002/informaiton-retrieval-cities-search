mahabubnagar is a city in mahabubnagar district of the indian state of telangana named after the 6th nizam, mir mahabub ali khan. it is the headquarters of mahbubnagar mandal in mahabubnagar revenue division. the city is also the largest in the district with an area of 39.64 km (15.31 sq mi) and 7th most populous in the state.
