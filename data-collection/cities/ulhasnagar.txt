ulhasnagar is a city in thane district, maharashtra, india. this city is a part of mumbai metropolitan region managed by mmrda. it had an estimated population of 506,098 at the 2011 census. ulhasnagar is a municipal city and the headquarters of the tahsil bearing the same name. it has a suburban station on the central line of the mumbai suburban railway.
