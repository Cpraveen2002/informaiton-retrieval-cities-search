Demographics[edit]
demographics[edit]
as of 2011 census, machilipatnam had a population of 1,70,008. the total population constitutes 83,561 males and 86,447 females — a sex ratio of 1035 females per 1000 males. 13,778 children are in the age group of 0–6 years, of which 7,076 are boys and 6,702 are girls. the average literacy rate stands at 83.32% with 130,173 literates, significantly higher than the state average of 67.41%.
