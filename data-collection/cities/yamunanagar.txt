yamunanagar (pronunciation (help·info)), is a city and a municipal corporation in yamunanagar district in the indian state of haryana. this town is known for the cluster of plywood units and paper industries. it provides timber to larger industries. the older town is called jagadhri. the yamunanagar-jagadhri railway station (yjud) services the city. despite its name, jagadhri railway station is situated in yamunanagar. there is also another railway station called jagadhri workshop in yamunanagar.
