history[edit]
sikar had been the biggest thikana (estate) of the jaipur state. previously sikar was known as shekhawati pradesh. it was the capital of thikana sikar. sikar is surrounded by fortified walls consisting of seven "pols" (gates). these historic gates are named: bawari gate, fatehpuri gate, nani gate, surajpole gate, dujod gate old, dujod gate new, and chandpole gate. shekhawat rajputs were rulers of this region. nearby to sikar , in district of hanumangarh wrd department's sub office of cad there are three aen prashant abhishek and manish ji
