kamarhati
Transport[edit]
transport[edit]
rail[edit]
kamarhati has one important rail station nearby on sealdah-ranaghat line by the eastern railway suburban network: agarpara station,belgharia railway station.
road[edit]
the b.t. road which passes through the heart of the town provides ample road transport. additionally other roads are there for smooth transport, like graham road,agarpara station road, feeder road, d.d. mondal ghat road etc.
