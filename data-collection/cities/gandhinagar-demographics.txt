gandhinagar
Demographics
demographics
according to the 2011 census of india, gandhinagar had a population of 195,891. males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. gandhinagar has an average literacy rate of 77.11%. male literacy is 82%, and female literacy is 73%. in gandhinagar, 11% of the population is under 6 years of age. over 95% of the population of gandhinagar are hindus.
