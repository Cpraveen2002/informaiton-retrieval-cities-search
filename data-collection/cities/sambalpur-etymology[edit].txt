sambalpur
Etymology[edit]
etymology[edit]
maa samalei, the deity from whom the city derives its name
sambalpur derives its name from the goddess samalei (odia: ସମଲେଇ ମାଁ), who is regarded as the reigning deity of the region. the region in which sambalpur city is located was also known as hirakhanda from ancient times. historically it has also been known as "sambalaka". claudius ptolemy called it "sambalak".
