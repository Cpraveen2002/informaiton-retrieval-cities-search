bilaspur-chhattisgarh
Demographics[edit]
demographics[edit]
as of 2011 india census, bilaspur municipal corp had a population of 365,579. bilaspur urban area population was estimated at 652,851. in august 2019, adjacent 18 towns and sub-urban areas were included in the bilaspur municipal corporation. males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. bilaspur has an average literacy rate of 91.29%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 92.94% and female literacy of 88.33%. 15% of the population is under 6 years of age.
according to the 2011 census, bilaspur district, chhattisgarh has a population of 2,662,077, roughly equal to the nation of kuwait or the us state of nevada. bilaspur district ranks 152nd in india (out of a total of 640). the district has a population density of 322 inhabitants per square kilometre (830/sq mi) . its population growth rate over the decade 2001–2011 was 33.21%. bilaspur has a sex ratio of  972 females for every 1000 males, and a literacy rate of 71.59%.
the main languages spoken are chhattisgarhi, hindi.
