education[edit]
rishi bankim chandra college was established at naihati in 1947. rishi bankim chandra college for women was initially started as the morning section for women in rishi bankim chandra college. the college was rechristened in 1984 as rishi bankim chandra college for women. rishi bankim chandra evening college was set up initially as the evening section of rishi bankim chandra college and was rechristened as rishi bankim chandra evening college in 1984.
