demographics[edit]
as of the 2001 india census,
hinduism is majority religion in raichur with 57.61% followers. islam is second most popular religion in raichur with approximately 39.87% following it. in raichur, christianity is followed by 1.18%, jainism by 0.94%, sikhism by 0.08% and buddhism by 0.08%. around 0.00% stated 'other religion', approximately 0.29% stated 'no particular religion'. the official language is kannada, but urdu and telugu are also spoken in large proportion.
