vasai-virar mayor's marathon[edit]
sachin at vasai-virar mayor's marathon in 2014
ritesh deshmukh at vasai-virar mayor's marathon
vasai-virar mayor's marathon is annual marathon race organized in vasai-virar area. it began in 2011 and in 2012 became a national level running event, recognized by the afi. the event sees participation in excess of 15,000 across various categories and offers prize money in excess of rs 30 lakhs.
swimming pool[edit]
vvmc has built an olympic-size swimming pool in vasai & virar.
