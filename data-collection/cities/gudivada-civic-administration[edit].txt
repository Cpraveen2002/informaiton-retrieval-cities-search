Civic Administration[edit]
civic administration[edit]
gudivada municipality was constituted in the year 1937 as a "third grade municipality". it was upgraded to "special grade municipality" and is spread over an area of 12.67 km (4.89 sq mi) with 36 election wards. ysr congress party won the gudivada municipal elections in 2014 and the present mla is kodali sri venkateswara rao.
