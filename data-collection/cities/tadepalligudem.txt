tadepalligudem is a city in west godavari district of the indian state of andhra pradesh. it is a selection grade municipality and the mandal headquarters of tadepalligudem mandal in bhimavaram revenue division. tadepalligudem is the second biggest city after bhimavaram in west godavari district as per 2011 census in terms of population.
