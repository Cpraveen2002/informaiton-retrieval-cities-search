demographics[edit]
as of 2011 census of india, the town had a population of 227,533. the total population constitute, 111,596 males and 115,937 females —a sex ratio of 1039 females per 1,000 males, higher than the national average of 940 per 1,000. 20,487 children are in the age group of 0–6 years, of which 10,495 are boys and 9,992 are girls. the average literacy rate stands at 81.85% with 169,461 literates, significantly higher than the national average of 73.00%.
telugu is the official language.
