Demographics[edit]
demographics[edit]
as of 2011 india census, bhiwani had a population of 196,057. males constitute 54% of the population and females 46%. bhiwani has an average literacy rate of 69%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 76% and female literacy of 62%. 13% of the population is under 7 years of age. the city consists of every religion and caste major rajputs and jats.
