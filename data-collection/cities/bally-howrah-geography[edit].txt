Geography[edit]
geography[edit]
bally is located at  wikiminiatlas22°39′n 88°20′e﻿ / ﻿22.65°n 88.34°e﻿ / 22.65; 88.34 and also the northernmost part of howrah city. it has an average elevation of 15 metres (49 feet). it is more elevated in the west than the east (where flows the hoogly river), thus leading to a problem of water logging. bally is separated from uttarpara, hooghly by bally canal (bally khal) at its north. a bridge was constructed over bally canal in 1846 to join the two localities.
