international relations[edit]
coimbatore has sister city relationship with toledo, ohio. the relation has enabled exchange in the fields of arts and education between the cities. a twin city pact with the german city of esslingen was signed in july 2016 enabling the two cities to collaborate on areas of mutual interest, health, education, culture and social development.
