raigarh
Education[edit]
education[edit]
the average literacy rate in raigarh district as per census 2011 is 85.22 % of which males and females are 92.01 % and 78.09 % literates respectively. as of the 2011 census of india, raigarh had 55 primary schools, 37 middle schools, 9 secondary schools and 16 senior-secondary school, along with 2 arts and commerce colleges (including kirodimal government arts and science college), 1 law college, 1 medical college(late shri lakhiram agrawal medical college), 1 polytechnic college. o.p. jindal university and kirodimal institute of technology are newer institutions in the city.
