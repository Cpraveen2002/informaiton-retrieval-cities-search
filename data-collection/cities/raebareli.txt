raebareli pronunciation (help·info) is a city in indian state of uttar pradesh. it is the administrative headquarters of raebareli district and a part of lucknow division. the city is situated on the banks of the sai river, 82 km (51 mi) southeast of lucknow. it possesses many architectural features and sites, chief of which is a strong and spacious fort. as of 2011, raebareli has a population of 191,316 people, in 35,197 households.
