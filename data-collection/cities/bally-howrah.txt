bally (bengali: বালি) is a neighbourhood in the city of howrah of howrah district in the indian state of west bengal. it is a part of the area covered by kolkata metropolitan development authority (kmda).
bally is under the jurisdiction of bally police station of howrah city police. it is governed by the bally municipality.
