as of the 2011 indian census, kaithal had a total population of 144,915, of which 76,794 were males and 68,121 were females. the population within the age group of 0 to 6 years was 17,531. the total number of literates in kaithal was 100,944, which constituted 69.7% of the population with a male literacy of 75.3% and a female literacy of 63.3%. the effective literacy rate of the 7+ population of kaithal was 79.2%, of which male literacy rate was 86.1% and the female literacy rate was 71.6%. the scheduled castes population was 24,760. kaithal had 28547 households in 2011.
hindi is the official language of kaithal. punjabi and english are additional official languages.
