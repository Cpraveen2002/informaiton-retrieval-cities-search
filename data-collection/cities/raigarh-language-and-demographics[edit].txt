language and demographics[edit]

see also: list of cities in chhattisgarh
languages spoken are chhattisgarhi hindi, and odia. the demographics of the city consist mainly of people from chhattisgarh, odisha, haryana and bihar. a sizeable community of bengali, telugu and marathi speaking people also live here.
as of 2001 india census, raigarh district had a population of 12,69,925, and the city of raigarh a population of 364,287. males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. raigarh has an average literacy rate of 71%, male literacy is 79%, and female literacy is 62%. about 80% of the population are hindus, 15% christians, and the remaining 5% are other religions. in raigarh, 13% of the population is under 6 years of age.
