gurgaon
Crime
crime
the city reported 89 homicides in 2012. the total vehicle thefts registered by the police in gurgaon in 2014 was 3,638.
the police department in gurgaon is headed by the commissioner of police - gurugram police, which forms a part of the haryana police. and reports to the haryana state government. in 2018 kk rao (ips) was appointed commissioner of police in gurgaon. gurugram police has a separate traffic police department headquartered in sector 51. fire protection within the city limits is provided by municipal corporation of gurugram through four fire stations, located in sector 29, sector 37, udyog vihar and bhim nagar.
