jehanabad is a town in nagar parishad and is the headquarters of jehanabad district in the indian state of bihar. jehanabad is famous for barabar hill caves these are the oldest surviving rock-cut caves in india, dating from the maurya empire (322–185 bce) some with ashokan inscriptions.
