narasaraopet
Transport[edit]
transport[edit]
the guntur to kurnool / ananthapuram  nh 544d bypasses the city and the nh167a road (piduguralla to chirala) passes through the city. the town has a total road length of 157.08 km (97.60 mi). apsrtc operates buses from narasaraopet bus station.
narasaraopet railway station is located on the nallapadu–nandyal section and administered under guntur railway division of south central railway zone.
