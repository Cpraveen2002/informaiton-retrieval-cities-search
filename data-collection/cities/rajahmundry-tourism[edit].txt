the river godavari and its bridges and the sir arthur cotton museum are some of the attractions in the city. rajahmundry is a tourist destination in the state. daily tourist boats are availed from pushkar ghat to papikondalu and bhadrachalam. nurseries in kadiam mandal is another tourist destination.
government of andhra pradesh is rehabilitating the old godavari bridge (havelock bridge) and constructing resorts, convention centers, film studios, hotels, recreational places at pichukalanka and other islands on river godavari and eco-tourism centre at kadiyapulanka, zoological park. government of andhra pradesh is constructing rajahmundry international cricket stadium on ppp mode to host international matches. greater rajamahendravaram municipal corporation (grmc), godavari urban development authority (guda) and andhra pradesh tourism development corporation (aptdc) is planning river front development on 22 km (14 mi) stretch of river godavari from venkatanagaram to vemagiri. the government is signing mous with private agencies for malls, multiplexes, hotels, convention centers and recreational places on ppp mode as a part of tourism development. rajahmundry airport is being developed as an international airport for giving a major boost to tourism in godavari districts. polavaram project, which is 25 km (16 mi) away from city will become another major tourist site near the city.
