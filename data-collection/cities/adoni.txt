adoni is a city in the kurnool district in the indian state of andhra pradesh. it is a municipality and the headquarters of adoni mandal, administered under the adoni revenue division. in the 2011 census of india, adoni had a population of 166,344, making it the 15th most populous town in the state with an urban agglomeration population of 184,625.
