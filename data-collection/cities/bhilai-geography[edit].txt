bhilai
Geography[edit]
geography[edit]
bhilai lies at 21.21°n 81.38°e in central india at an elevation of 297m above sea level on the banks of the shivnath river, a tributary of the river mahanadi. it covers an area of 341 km (132 sq mi).
according to the bureau of indian standards, the town falls under seismic zone 2, in a scale of 2 to 5 (in order of increasing value of vulnerability to earthquakes).
