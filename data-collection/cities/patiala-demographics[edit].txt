demographics[edit]
as per provisional data of the 2021 census, patiala ua had a population of 820,000 and patiala city 763,280. males constituted 54% of the population, and females 46%. patiala had an average literacy rate of 86%, higher than the national average of 64.9%. in patiala, 10% of the population was under 5 years of age.
