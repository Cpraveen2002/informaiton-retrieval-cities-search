burhanpur also known as brampore as is a city in the indian state of madhya pradesh. it is the administrative seat of burhanpur district. it is situated on the north bank of the tapti river and 340 kilometres (211 mi) southwest of the state's capital city of bhopal. the city is a municipal corporation.
