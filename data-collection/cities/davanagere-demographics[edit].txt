demographics[edit]
as of 2011 census, davanagere city had a population of 435,125. males constitute 52% of the population, and females 48%. davanagere has an average literacy rate of 85%, higher than the national average of 74.04%: male literacy is 89% and, female literacy is 81%. in davanagere, 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
