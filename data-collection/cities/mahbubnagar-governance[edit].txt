governance[edit]
mahbub nagar municipality is the civic body of the city, which was constituted in 1942 as a third grade municipality. it was upgraded to second grade in 1959, to first grade in 1983 and finally to special grade municipality in 2004. it is spread over an area of 98.64 km (38.09 sq mi). mahbub nagar urban agglomeration consists of mahbub nagar municipality, census towns of boyapalle, yenugonda and the out growths of mahbub nagar (rural) (full), yedira (part) village.
