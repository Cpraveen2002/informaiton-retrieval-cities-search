hazaribagh is a city and a municipal corporation in hazaribagh district in the indian state of jharkhand. it is the divisional headquarters of north chotanagpur division. it is considered as a health resort and is also popular for hazaribagh wildlife sanctuary (17 kilometres [11 mi] from city). it is represented in the indian lok sabha by its member of parliament jayant sinha.
