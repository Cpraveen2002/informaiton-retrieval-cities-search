bahraich
Geography and climate[edit]
geography and climate[edit]
it has an average elevation of 126 metres (413 feet). bahraich has a warm humid subtropical climate with  hot summers from april to july. the rainy season is from july to mid-september when bahraich gets an average rainfall from the south-west monsoon winds, and occasionally frontal rainfall will occur in january. in winter the maximum temperature is around 25 °c (77 °f) and the minimum is in the −1 to 7 °c (30 to 45 °f) range. fog is quite common from late december to late january. summers are extremely hot with temperatures rising to the 40 to 47 °c (104 to 117 °f) range, the average highs being in the high of 30s (degree celsius). average annual rainfall is 1,900 centimetres (750 inches) (approx).
