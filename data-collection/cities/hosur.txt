hosur is an industrial city located in the indian state of tamil nadu. hosur is one of the  municipal corporations in tamil nadu. it is located on the bank of the river river ponnaiyar, 40 kilometres (25 mi) southeast of bengaluru and 306 kilometres (190 mi) west of chennai, the state capital. hosur is home to major manufacturing industries including stellantis, ashok leyland, titan, tvs motors, caterpillar, ather energy, sundaram fasteners, schaeffler, and many others.
