religions in sambhal[edit]
sambhal is muslim majority city in india with approximately 77.67 % of city population following islam as their religion. hinduism is second most common religion in city of sambhal with approximately 22.00 % following it. in sambhal city, christianity is followed by 0.12 %, jainism by 0.02 %, buddhism by 0.03 % and sikhism by 0.06 %. around 0.00 % stated 'other religion', approximately 0.09 % stated 'no particular religion'.
