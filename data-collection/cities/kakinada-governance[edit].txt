Governance[edit]
governance[edit]
kakinada municipal corporation is spread over an area of 16.63 km (6.42 sq mi), while the urban agglomeration area of kakinada spreads over an area of 40.36 km (15.58 sq mi). the urban agglomeration constituents include the areas of kakinada municipal corporation, census towns of chidiga, ramanayyapeta, suryaraopeta and the out growths of ganganapalle, sarpavaram, vakalapudi and turangi.
