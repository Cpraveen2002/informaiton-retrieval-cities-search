State assembly constituency[edit]
state assembly constituency[edit]
avadi is a state assembly constituency in tamil nadu, india, formed after constituency delimitation. the areas included are poonamalle taluk (partially), pattabiram, thirunindravur, tiruverkadu and avadi (m). it is included in the thiruvallur parliamentary constituency.
