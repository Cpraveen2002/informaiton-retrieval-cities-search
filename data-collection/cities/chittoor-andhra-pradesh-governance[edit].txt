governance[edit]
chittoor municipal corporation is the civic administrative body of the city. it was constituted as a grade–iii municipality in the year 1917. it was upgraded to grade–ii in 1950, grade–i in 1965, special grade in 1980 and selection grade in 2000. on 7 july 2012, it was upgraded to municipal corporation by merging 14 gram panchayats into the corporation and is spread over an area of 69.75 km (26.93 sq mi).
