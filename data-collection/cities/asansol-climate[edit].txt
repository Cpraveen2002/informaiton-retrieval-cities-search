climate[edit]
asansol has hot and dry summers and mild winters. in summers temperatures soar above 45 °c with dry hot air blowing known as 'loo' while in winter temperatures go below 10 °c. monsoon lasts from june to september. the köppen climate classification sub-type for this climate is "aw" (tropical savanna climate).
