jodhpur
Sports[edit]
sports[edit]
jodhpur has two outdoor stadiums and one indoor stadium complex. barkatullah khan stadium has hosted two cricket one day internationals. maharaja umaid singh stadium and gaushala maidan sports complex are also among other sports facilities. the city has a well developed polo ground where tournaments are held occasionally.
