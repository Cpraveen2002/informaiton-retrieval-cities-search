Demographics[edit]
demographics[edit]
as per the 2011 census of india kulti municipal area had a total population of 313,809, of which 163,193 (52%) were males and 150,616 (48%) were females. population below 6 years was 35,385. the total number of literates in kulti was 209,952 (88.72% of the population over 6 years).
as of 2001 india census, kulti had a population of 290,057. males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. kulti has an average literacy rate of 62%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 70%, and female literacy is 52%. in kulti, 12% of the population is under 6 years of age. according to the census, 19% of the population in kulti is muslim, 70% hindu. other minorities such as christian, sikhs, buddhist and jain constitute the rest of the city's population [1]
