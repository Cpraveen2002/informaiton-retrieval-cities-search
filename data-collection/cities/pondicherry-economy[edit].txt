economy[edit]
in 2012, the ministry of power inaugurated the smart grid project in puducherry. farming around pondicherry include crops such as rice, pulses, sugarcane, coconuts, and cotton. in 2016, the pondicherry state government employees central federation presented a status paper on the fiscal and social crisis in puducherry to union home minister rajnath singh. the report stated that a "combination of a staggering debt, stagnant tax revenues and rampant misappropriation of funds has throttled the economy of the union territory" and called for measures on a war footing to "deliver good governance and end corruption."
