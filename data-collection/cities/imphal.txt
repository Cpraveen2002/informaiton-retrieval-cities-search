imphal (meitei pronunciation: /im.pʰal/; english pronunciation: /ˈɪmfəl/) is the capital city of the indian state of manipur. the metropolitan centre of the city contains the ruins of kangla palace (also known as kangla fort), the royal seat of the former kingdom of manipur, surrounded by a moat. spread over parts of the districts of imphal west and imphal east, the former contains the majority of the city's area and population. imphal is part of the smart cities mission under the ministry of housing and urban affairs.
