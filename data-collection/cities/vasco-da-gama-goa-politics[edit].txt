Politics[edit]
politics[edit]
the city of vasco falls under the boundaries of the south goa (lok sabha constituency) previously mormugao lok sabha constituency in the lok sabha. the current member of parliament is francisco sardinha of the indian national congress.
the extended city of vasco / mormugao elects three representatives to the goa legislative assembly:
mormugao: the current mla is sankalp amonkar of the bjp. vasco da gama: the current mla is krishna salkar of the bjp. dabolim: the current mla is mauvin godinho of the bjp.
