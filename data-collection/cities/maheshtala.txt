maheshtala (bengali: [məɦeːʃt̪əlɑː]) is a city and a municipality of the south 24 parganas district in the indian state of west bengal. it is situated on the eastern banks of the hooghly river. it is a part of the area covered by the kolkata metropolitan development authority (kmda).
