civic administration[edit]
dharmavaram municipality was formed on 1 april 1964 as a grade–iii municipality. it is upgraded to selection grade and has a jurisdictional area of 42.50 km (16.41 sq mi) with 40 election wards. each ward is represented by a ward member and the ward committee is headed by a chairperson. the present chairperson of the municipality is beere gopala krishna and the commissioner is v. mallikarjun.
