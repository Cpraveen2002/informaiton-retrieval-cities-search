vijayawada
Awards[edit]
awards[edit]
the swachh survekashan 2021 has identified vijayawada as the third cleanest city in the nation. in the category of cities with more than 10 lakh inhabitants, the city rose from sixth place in 2020 to third place this year. a five-star designation for "garbage free city" has also been given to vijayawada. such a good ranking can be accredited to policy measures such as the introduction of waste disposal vehicles in the city by n. chandrababu naidu
