mahbubnagar
Transport[edit]
transport[edit]
mahabubnagar railway station
the city is connected to major cities and towns by means of road, railway and airway. national and state highways that pass through the city are nh 44 and nh 167, state highway 20 and 23. tsrtc operates buses to various destinations from mahbub nagar bus station of the city. the city has rail connectivity with the presence of mahbub nagar main station, mahbub nagar town, yenugonda station and diviti pally stations. rajiv gandhi international airport is only 75 kms from the city.
